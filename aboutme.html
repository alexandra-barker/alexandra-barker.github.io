About Me

My name is Alexandra Barker.

Professional Background

I graduated with my Bachelor of Science from the United States Military Academy at West Point, and commissioned into the Army as a Human Resources officer. After my term of service was up, I moved into technical recruiting. I was faced with a decision I wasn't used to having: what I wanted to do with my career? The Army tells you what they need you to do, your preference doesn't usually factor into it. After talking with friends and mentors, I decided to pursue data science. I enrolled in Correlation One's Data Science bootcamp to explore the concepts of data science before embarking on the time and expense commitment of a Master's program, and I was happy to learn that the concepts came easily. I completed one of the most ambitious projects in the course and my instructors highly recommended me for a Master's program to deepen my education.

I currently work as a Program Manager at Lowe's Companies, Inc. I oversee military and veteran hiring and strategic initiatives, focusing on initiatives within Corporate, Technology, and Student Veterans. I manage our SkillBridge internship/fellowship program within our corporate office.

Berkeley MIDS Program

I started the Master of Information and Data Science (MIDS) program at Berkeley in the Fall of 2023. In my coursework, I have studied programming, research design, data engineering, statistics, machine learning, data visualization, natural language processing, and causal inference. I hope to continue and focus on generative artificial intelligence and large language models. I am projected to graduate in May 2025.

Personal Life

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my husband and my dog, Finn, the cutest corgi in the world. In the summer, you can usually find us paddle boarding on the lake or trying somewhere new for brunch. I also enjoy chess, long-distance running, and pilates. Current binges include Love Island (USA, UK, and Aus), Selling Sunset, and 90's/00's teen movies. Notable accomplishments include being in the top 5% of Taylor Swift listeners in my Spotify Wrapped last year (my music taste was categorized as "Eclectic"...I'm strangely proud of it).
